Available Arts Courses

Visual Arts: Drawing and Painting

Develop artistic skills through various mediums including pencil, charcoal, watercolor, and acrylic. Learn fundamental techniques and express your creativity.

Visual Arts: Sculpture and Ceramics

Work with clay, wire, and other materials to create three-dimensional art. Explore pottery, hand-building, and sculptural techniques.

Music Theory

Understand the language of music through study of rhythm, melody, harmony, and composition. Learn to read and write musical notation.

Instrumental Music

Learn to play an instrument through structured lessons. Develop technical skills, musicianship, and performance abilities.

Vocal Music and Choir

Develop singing technique, breath control, and vocal health. Perform in ensemble settings and explore various musical styles.

Drama and Theater Arts

Explore acting techniques, improvisation, stagecraft, and theatrical production. Build confidence through performance.

Dance

Study various dance styles including ballet, jazz, modern, and hip-hop. Develop coordination, expression, and choreography skills.

Digital Arts and Design

Create digital artwork using modern tools and software. Learn graphic design, digital illustration, and basic animation techniques.
