Available Mathematic Courses

Pre-Algebra

This covers proportional relationships, rational numbers, and algebraic expressions and equations. The difficulty of this course is typically considered 7th grade level.

Multi-Digit Multiplication and Division

This covers multi-digit multiplication and division: including fractions and decimals. The difficulty of this course is typically considered 4th grade level.

Simple Arithmetic

This covers basic counting, addition, subtraction, and introduction to multiplication. The difficulty of this course is typically considered 1st-2nd grade level.

Algebra I

Learn to solve linear equations, work with polynomials, and understand functions. Typically considered 8th-9th grade level.

Geometry

Explore shapes, angles, theorems, and proofs. Study area, volume, and spatial reasoning. Typically considered 9th-10th grade level.

Algebra II

Advanced algebra including quadratic equations, exponential functions, and logarithms. Typically considered 10th-11th grade level.
