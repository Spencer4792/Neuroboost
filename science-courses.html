Available Science Courses

General Science

Introduction to scientific thinking and the scientific method. Explore basic concepts across all science disciplines through hands-on experiments.

Biology

Study living organisms, cells, genetics, evolution, and ecosystems. Learn about human body systems and the diversity of life on Earth.

Chemistry

Explore matter, elements, compounds, chemical reactions, and the periodic table. Conduct safe laboratory experiments.

Physics

Understand motion, forces, energy, electricity, magnetism, and waves. Apply mathematical concepts to real-world phenomena.

Earth Science

Study geology, meteorology, oceanography, and astronomy. Learn about Earth's systems, weather patterns, and our place in the universe.

Environmental Science

Examine ecosystems, conservation, climate change, and sustainability. Understand human impact on the environment.

Anatomy and Physiology

Detailed study of human body structure and function. Explore organ systems and how they work together to maintain health.
